Notes of English Learning

2.7

Part 1

  1. encompass

    • v. 包含；包围

    • e.g. Russia encompasses over 100 languages spread across 11 time zones.

      • time zone(s) n. 时区。

  2. Russia is more than double the size of the continental United States, 17 million square kilometers, which makes up one eighth of total habitable area on earth.

    • ...square kilometer(s) n. ...平方公里
    • one eighth (of) n. 八分之一
    • habitable area on earth n. 地球上适宜栖居的区域

  3. mind-blowing

    • adj. extremely impressive, exciting or shocking.

  4. border

    • n. 边界；国界。

  5. flat grassland

    • 平的草原

  6. pillage

    • v. 掠夺 n. 掠夺物
    • e.g. You are an army looking to pillage land.

  7. horse-riding warrior(s)

    • n. 骑兵

  8. gunpowder

    • n. 火药

  9. way too big

    • adj. 超级大
    • There is only so long you can keep an empire like this together. Way too big.

  1. One thing is for sure, said Moscow, which was soon becoming the Russia empire, "we're not gonna let anyone sneak up on us again like Mongols did!"

    • sneak up (on) vi.偷偷走近，此处引申为偷袭，侵略领土。

  2. I watched the long-waited episode 15 of TV series Reset with my girlfriend.

    • long-waited 久等的，形容等了很久的事物，比如开端大结局。

  3. zoom out/in

    • vi, 拉远距离看/ 拉近距离看，常用于相机变焦；zoom in又可引申为聚焦某事物的细节。

  1. keep track of...

    • v. 追踪，记录
    • e.g. I can label my versions so I can (sort of) keep track of them.

  2. crater

    • n. 弹坑
    • e.g. The moon is covered in craters.
    • cratered terrain 坑坑洼洼的地形

  3. contamination

    • n. 污染
    • radioactive contamination 辐射污染，尤指核辐射。

  4. isotopic abundance(s)

    • n. 同位素丰度

  5. There is still a lot to learn (up there). (指moon)

    • a lot to learn, learn主动表被动，表示月球上还有很多需要学习的东西。
Part 2

  1. an irresistible trend of...

    • 一个...的必然趋势

  2. cannot bear closer analysis / cannot hold water

    • 经不起推敲

  3. compensate for

    • v. 弥补，赔偿，往往指的是事后补偿，使坏的事物变好。类似于雪中送炭。
    • 区别：与这个词经常弄混：complement（有动词词性）表示补充某个东西使其更好更完美，使好的事物更好。类似于锦上添花。

  4. bear in mind that = remember

  5. He works less enthusiastically than Peter works.

    • 这里than... 引导状语从句

  6. Life over there is getting:

    • more and more difficult. 越来越困难
    • increasingly difficult.

  7. He works three times as hard as I (work).

    • 也是一个比较结构，as后面用 I 而不是 me 是因为这是个状语从句省略了谓语。

    • 其他用法

      • as possible as I can 状语结构，放于句尾表示尽我所能。

      • as far as it goes 就目前来说

      • as far as possible 尽可能

      • as far as I know 据我所知

      • as fast as possible 尽快

        • e.g. We'd appreciate your leaving as fast as possible.

          如果您能尽快离开，我们将不胜感激。
